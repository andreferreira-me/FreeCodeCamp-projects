About me

Hi, my name is André Ferreira. I am a entrepreneur, web developer and digital marketer living in Curitiba, Paraná, Brazil.

Student of Internet Systems at Positive Technology Center, graduated in Social Communication with specialization in Advertising and Marketing at UNINTER and certified by Google in Google Analytics.

Spend my days working in some different areas such as back-end development (.NET and PHP), front-end development (HTML5, CSS3 and Javascript), search engine optimization (SEO) and digital marketing. I'm also interested in blockchain technologies such as Bitcoin and Ethereum, IoT, robotics, artificial intelligence and other geek stuff.

In the past, I worked in Mirum agency improving big national websites for search engines and previously, I worked in a smaller agency, Excom, which performed the management of social media and sponsored links campaigns, besides the production of graphic elements and texts for various clients.

I am extremely passionate about web development and Internet in all its forms, helping small businesses and professionals to build and improve their online presence and develop solutions according to your needs. Besides the Internet, I love people, to travel, read about various subjects (self-knowledge, astronomy, anthropology, philosophy, history, etc) and I try to lead a less sedentary life running at the park, practicing boxing, swimming or surfing when I have free time.
